ABOUT ME

SORIKA WOLF

Hey there! Sorika here! Wow, what a ride this has been! From a single-parent home in Quebec City, Canada, I moved to NYC with big dreams and little money - as so many of us do.

A tiny dorm-room in the Stratford Arms is where my journey began, and I am grateful for every moment since then.

From fast-paced procedural dramas, to cheery upbeat Hallmark sets, to the high-art dark comedy Noah Hawley-led Fargo, to the fresh and flirty set of Kissing is the Easy Part on Tubi, I have been gifted with opportunities to stretch and grow my craft with every job.

I love what I do and look forward to sharing it all with you.
